How this course works

Each week is a standalone lesson with theory, daily 30-minute practice plans, a real song to play, and a checklist of mastery targets. Start at Week 1 and work through in order — every skill builds on the one before it. By Week 12 you will have three complete song arrangements, a working ear for harmony, and the ability to play along with any song within 15 seconds of hearing it.

🔢

Number System

Think in numbers 1–7, not note names. Transpose to any key instantly. The same system professional session musicians use.

👂

Playing by Ear

Find any melody, identify any key, play along with any song — all by ear. No sheet music ever required.

🎵

Real Songs

Every week features a real, popular song as the practical target. Beatles, Scorpions, Armstrong, Cohen, Metallica and more.

🎹

30 Min / Day

Every session is structured to fit in exactly 30 minutes. Timed activities, clear goals, no filler.

What you need
Any keyboard with at least 4 octaves. A sustain pedal is helpful from Week 11 but not required. No headphones needed.
⏱️
30 minutes a day
Every session is timed. Each activity tells you exactly how many minutes to spend. No session runs over 30 minutes.
📋
Follow in order
Each week builds directly on the one before. Don't skip ahead — the skills compound. Week 5 is only possible because of Weeks 1–4.
👂
No sheet music. Ever.
Everything in this course is learned by ear using the Nashville Number System. You will never need to read a note of sheet music.
