What this week is about

When you play 1–5–6m–4 in G major and it sounds exactly like C major — just higher — you will feel the number system click. That moment of recognition is the entire point of this course. The Axis in G: G–D–Em–C. Play it — same emotional arc as C major, just one step higher. That sameness is the number system working exactly as promised.

Ⓗ Major vs Minor Keys — Hearing the Difference

Is the Song “Home” on a Major or Minor Chord?

A song can centre on a major chord (bright, warm, resolved) or a minor chord (darker, more intense). Listen for where the melody feels finished. If that note belongs to a major chord, you’re in a major key. If it belongs to a minor chord, you’re in a minor key.

A minor key song uses the 6m chord from the relative major as its new home. A minor loop: Am–F–C–G — these are exactly the chords 6m–4–1–5 from C major. You already know all of them. Minor keys are not new chords; they are new perspectives on the same chords.

Major key→warm, settled, “arriving” — e.g. Wonderful Tonight
Minor key→searching, intense, darker — e.g. Billie Jean, Hotel California

Teacher's Notes

🗝️

G Major Is the Test

If the number system is real, G major feels instantly logical. If you’re still thinking in letter names rather than numbers, now is the time to commit fully.

♯

Embrace the Black Key

F# in G major is a landmark, not a problem. That one black key tells your hand “you’re in G major.” Every key’s black key pattern will eventually feel like home.

👁️

Minor Keys Are Just Perspective

You haven’t learned new chords for minor keys. Am–F–C–G uses chords you already know. The only difference is which one you call home.

🎯

The 2m→5→1 Is Sacred

Every time you play Dm→G→C or Am→D→G, your ears are learning the deepest grammar of tonal music. This movement is in every jazz standard, Motown hit, and gospel song ever written.

📝

Keep Your Song Log

Students who write down progressions they hear — even wrong guesses — improve fastest. “I thought it was 1–4–5 but it was 1–5–6m–4.” That awareness is the learning.

⚡

Resist More Chords

Fluency in 5 chords across 2 keys is worth far more than shallow knowledge of 10 chords. Solid foundation now means Week 8 feels like flying.
